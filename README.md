# Cherry 

AI-powered awareness and diagnostic support for women's health. Cherry provides personalized guidance, raises awareness, and supports research for menstrual and reproductive health conditions like PCOD, endometriosis, and related disorders.  

---

## Features

- **Symptom Checker** – AI-powered triage system to guide next steps  
- **Period Tracker** – Track cycles, symptoms, and health patterns  
- **Education & Resources** – Evidence-based information in plain language  
- **Research Collaboration** – Anonymous data sharing to improve diagnosis  

---

## Our Mission

Bridge the gap in diagnosis, awareness, and research for women's health by providing safe, transparent, and personalized digital support.  

---

## The Problem

- **6–13%** prevalence of PCOD in reproductive-aged women, yet up to **70% undiagnosed**  
- **6–8 years** median delay for endometriosis diagnosis  
- **Research Gaps** – Women’s health is underfunded relative to disease burden  

---

## Our Solution

Cherry combines education, symptom assessment, and research collaboration:  

- **Awareness & Education** – Accessible information for understanding  
- **Safe Triage System** – AI-driven guidance for self-care, GP visits, specialist consultation, or emergency care  
- **Evidence Transparency** – Clear labeling of evidence-based vs limited evidence  
- **Research Collaboration** – Anonymous data to support future diagnostic tools  

---

## Core Features

### Symptom Intake Form
Structured questionnaire covering age, cycle patterns, pain, fertility concerns, acne, weight changes, family history, and medications. Free-text responses are mapped to medical concepts using NLP.  

**Data collected:**  
- Age, cycle length, regularity  
- Pain patterns & severity  
- Fertility & lifestyle impacts  
- Medical history & medications  

### Safety Triage Rules
- 🟢 **Low Risk** – Monitor and educate  
- 🟡 **Moderate Risk** – Doctor referral  
- 🔴 **Red Flags** – Immediate care  

### AI Risk Scoring
Explainable ML model providing risk scores for:  
- PCOD/PCOS  
- Endometriosis  
- Thyroid disorders  
- Dysmenorrhea & Fibroids  

### Education & Resources
- Risk score explanations  
- Questions for clinicians  
- Vetted educational links  
- Limited evidence labels

## Who We Serve

**Primary Users:** People who menstruate (15–45) seeking awareness or experiencing symptoms  
- Undiagnosed individuals  
- Early awareness seekers  
- People with family history  

**Secondary Users:** Healthcare professionals, educators, NGOs, researchers  
- Primary care clinicians  
- Health educators and NGOs  
- Researchers improving diagnosis

## Unique Approach

- **AI-Powered Awareness** – Intelligent triage for underdiagnosed conditions

## Privacy & Consent

- Symptom data is encrypted; no identifiable data collected  
- Anonymous data may be used for research  
- Participation is optional; withdraw consent anytime  
- Tool does **not** replace professional medical diagnosis
